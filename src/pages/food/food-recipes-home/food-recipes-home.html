Veggie Bowl

Ingrédients:

-Chinese noodles -rice vinegar -soya sauce -lawyer -tomatoes -carrots -cucumber -chickpea Pour le houmous: -pois chiches -zeste de citron -basilic séché -menthe séchée

Preparation:

* After cooking the Chinese noodles (4 minutes over low heat) and cooling them, I put some rice vinegar, soy sauce and dried shallots in it. All in very limited quantity. * Then I cut all my vegetables in strips and scrape the carrots. * I place the noodles at the bottom of the bowl and put my vegetables on top. * For hummus I mix my chickpeas with a little soy cream to which I add salt, dried basil and mint and a zest of lemon. I mix until I get a creamy dough and I place it in the center of the bowl, it is he who will taste my dish and serve sauce. * I add a little basil on top. * I prepare a soy sauce with herbs in it to pour a few drops at my convenience during the tasting and I enjoy .... :)
